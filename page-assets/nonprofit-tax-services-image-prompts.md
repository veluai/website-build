# Nonprofit Tax Services Image Prompts

Production-source record only. Final filenames, hosted URLs, dimensions, accessibility treatment, and implementation status are governed by the Nonprofit Tax Services image inventory.

## Shared Visual Standard

Every prompt should require:

* Clean, modern vector illustration
* Same visual family as the approved Velu service-page illustrations
* Professional nonprofit financial-advisory aesthetic
* White background
* Dark navy blue primary linework and shapes
* Light gray secondary elements
* Restrained use of muted accent colors
* Clean flat-vector or lightly dimensional vector treatment
* Consistent stroke weight
* Rounded, professional forms
* Clear visual hierarchy
* Generous white space
* Calm, trustworthy, organized tone
* High-resolution output
* No photorealism
* No stock-photo appearance
* No cartoonish exaggeration
* No gradients that conflict with the existing Velu illustration family
* No unnecessary decorative elements
* No government logos, IRS seals, or official insignia
* No warning, penalty, audit, courtroom, or enforcement imagery
* No implication that approval is guaranteed
* No implication that bookkeeping cleanup, reconciliation, or financial-statement preparation is included in standard Form 990 preparation

When a Form 990 document appears, use only the clearly legible title:

FORM 990

Avoid dense or small text inside documents. Use abstract lines, checkboxes, charts, or simple symbols for the remainder of the document content.

## Prompt-Control Notes

For all generated assets:

* Verify every visible word before approval
* Prefer no embedded text except where specifically approved
* Reject and regenerate any image containing misspelled, distorted, invented, or unreadable text
* Do not rely on small document text to communicate the concept
* Communicate primarily through composition, symbols, and visual hierarchy
* Preserve consistency across all five images
* Treat the approved image brief as the controlling source if any prompt wording appears ambiguous
