## Alt-Text Quality Check

The approved alt text for the four content images:

* Describes the meaningful visual content.
* Reflects the image's page purpose.
* Uses natural language.
* Does not begin with "image of," "graphic of," or "illustration of."
* Does not repeat the entire nearby heading.
* Does not keyword-stuff.
* Does not claim services outside the approved scope.
* Does not reference excluded form types or services.
* Does not imply guaranteed tax-exempt approval.
* Does not state that Velu independently verifies the client's underlying financial records.
