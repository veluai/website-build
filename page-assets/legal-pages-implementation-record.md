## Confirmed Website Practices Relevant To Privacy

- GoHighLevel is used for website pages, CRM, forms, scheduling, chat, email automation, and SMS.
- Discovery-call scheduling is handled through GoHighLevel.
- Form submissions are stored in GoHighLevel.
- Chat messages and chat histories are stored.
- Automated confirmation and follow-up emails are sent.
- Newsletter and marketing email signup exists.
- SMS may be used for reminders and marketing.
- Marketing texts are sent only after explicit checkbox consent.
- SMS consent is recorded in GoHighLevel.
- STOP opt-out functionality works.
- Expanded SMS disclosure language will not be implemented at this time.
- The current SMS consent experience does not include all of the following: message frequency language, message and data rates language, HELP instructions, "Consent is not a condition of purchase," direct Privacy Policy link beside the checkbox, or direct Terms and Conditions link beside the checkbox.
- Google Analytics is active.
- Meta Pixel and other advertising pixels are active.
- Cookies beyond strictly necessary platform cookies are used.
- No cookie banner will be implemented at this time.
- No cookie-category controls will be implemented at this time.
- No reject-nonessential option will be implemented at this time.
- Google Analytics and Meta Pixel are not blocked until consent.
- No cookie-preference center exists.
- No Global Privacy Control recognition is documented.
- No Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information link exists.
- Velu may purchase or obtain lead or contact data from third parties.
- Stripe processes website payment information.
- Complete payment-card numbers are submitted directly to Stripe.
- Velu may receive transaction status, billing, and limited payment details but does not receive or store complete payment-card numbers.
- A third-party client portal is used.
- The client-portal provider is not named in current documentation.
- No embedded video provider is currently used.
- Professional-service, accounting, tax, engagement, payment, and compliance records may generally be retained for seven years or longer where required.
- Marketing, prospect, form, and chat data may be retained until deleted or no longer needed.
- Consent and opt-out records may be retained to document compliance.
- The website is not directed to children under 13.
- Velu does not knowingly seek personal information from children under 13.
- Velu does not sell personal information for monetary consideration.
- Velu may share identifiers, device information, browsing activity, and interaction data with analytics and advertising partners for measurement, targeted advertising, and cross-context behavioral advertising.
- Privacy requests are submitted by email to `info@velu.us`.
