## Visual System

Use the same clean vector-illustration family as the other Velu service pages.

General style requirements for all images:

* Clean vector illustration
* Professional nonprofit advisory branding
* White background
* Navy blue primary palette
* Light gray supporting elements
* Minimal accent color usage
* Landscape orientation unless otherwise noted
* High resolution
* Minimal embedded text
* Consistent with existing Velu website illustration assets

Do not use:

* Fear-based tax imagery
* Penalty or warning symbolism
* Courtroom, audit, or enforcement visuals
* IRS seals, official government branding, or guaranteed-approval imagery
* Unapproved service claims
* Imagery implying bookkeeping cleanup is part of the standard Form 990 engagement
