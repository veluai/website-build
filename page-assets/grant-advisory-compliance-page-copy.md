## Grant Funding Creates Opportunity—and Complexity

Section heading:

Grant Funding Creates Opportunity—and Complexity

Left-column copy:

Grant funding can help nonprofits launch programs, expand services, and reach more people. But it also creates new financial responsibilities that can quickly stretch limited staff capacity.

Reporting deadlines, reimbursement timing, restricted funding requirements, and compliance obligations can create uncertainty even when programs are succeeding.

Question-card heading:

Questions nonprofit leaders need to answer:

Questions:

- Are grant budgets aligned with the true cost of the work?
- Will reimbursement timing create cash flow pressure?
- Are restricted funds being used and tracked appropriately?
- Do we have the documentation required for reports and reimbursements?
- Are grant-funded programs sustainable if funding changes?

Closing statement:

Without strong grant financial management, organizations can face delayed reimbursements, compliance risk, staff burden, and uncertainty about future funding.

## Supporting the Full Financial Lifecycle of Grant Funding

Section heading:

Supporting the Full Financial Lifecycle of Grant Funding

Introductory copy:

Velu supports the financial side of grant funding from the earliest planning stages through reporting, reimbursement, compliance, and future funding readiness.

We help nonprofit leaders think earlier and more strategically—connecting funding opportunities, grant budgets, restricted funding, reporting requirements, reimbursement activity, and long-term sustainability.

### Grant Strategy & Funding Visibility

Understand current and future funding opportunities, identify risks, and evaluate how grant-dependent programs affect sustainability.

### Grant Budgeting & Planning

Build realistic grant budgets that connect funding requirements, program costs, and organizational priorities.

### Restricted Funding & Compliance

Manage reporting, reimbursements, restricted funding, documentation, and compliance responsibilities after an award.

### Funder Confidence & Sustainability

Demonstrate strong stewardship, strengthen funder trust, and improve readiness for future funding.

Lifecycle closing statement:

The goal is not simply to complete reports. It is to help leaders understand how grant funding supports programs, where financial risks may emerge, and how responsible stewardship strengthens future funding readiness.
