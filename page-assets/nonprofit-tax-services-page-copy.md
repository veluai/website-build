## Why It Matters

H2:

Protect Your Tax-Exempt Status With Timely, Accurate Filing

Body copy:

Maintaining tax-exempt status requires more than operating in support of your mission. Nonprofits must also meet annual federal filing requirements and provide complete, accurate information about their finances, programs, governance, and activities.

Form 990 brings that information together in one filing. It may be reviewed by the IRS, funders, board members, regulators, and members of the community as they evaluate how your organization operates and uses its resources.

A structured preparation process helps your organization meet its filing responsibilities, communicate its financial and governance information clearly, and protect its tax-exempt status without making the process more complicated than it needs to be.

The page must not state or imply that Velu provides:

* Form 990-PF preparation

* Advanced nonprofit tax planning

* Standalone IRS notice representation
* Bookkeeping cleanup as part of the standard Form 990 engagement
* Account reconciliations as part of the standard Form 990 engagement
* Financial statement preparation as part of the standard Form 990 engagement
