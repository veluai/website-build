## Protect Your Tax-Exempt Status Through Accurate Filing

Maintaining tax-exempt status requires more than operating in support of your mission. Nonprofits must also meet annual federal filing requirements and provide complete, accurate information about their finances, programs, governance, and activities.

Form 990 brings that information together in one filing. It may be reviewed by the IRS, funders, board members, regulators, and members of the community as they evaluate how your organization operates and uses its resources.

A structured preparation process helps your organization meet its filing responsibilities, communicate its financial and governance information clearly, and protect its tax-exempt status without making the process more complicated than it needs to be.

## Nonprofit Tax Services That Help Protect Your Tax-Exempt Status

Velu helps nonprofit organizations meet important federal filing requirements with accurate Form 990 preparation and support for organizations applying for or restoring tax-exempt status.

### Form 990 Preparation

Prepare and file Form 990, Form 990-EZ, or Form 990-N with support from a team that understands nonprofit financial reporting, governance disclosures, public-support requirements, and federal filing compliance.

Velu prepares the applicable return and schedules, assists with public-support testing and extensions when needed, and guides your organization through review and electronic filing.

### Tax-Exempt Status Applications

Navigate the federal tax-exemption application process with clear guidance on the financial, organizational, operational, and governance information required.

Velu supports initial applications and reinstatement engagements where appropriate, helping nonprofit leaders organize the necessary information and move through the process with greater clarity.
