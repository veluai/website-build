## Section 2: Included Services

## What's Included in Our Nonprofit Accounting Services

Your accounting team should do more than track transactions.

Our outsourced accounting services for nonprofits provide leaders with accurate financial information, clear reporting, and confidence in their organization's financial health.

### Financial Reporting

Clear, timely reporting that helps leadership make confident decisions.

Included items:

- Monthly financial statements
- Budget-to-actual reporting
- Board-ready reporting packages
- Cash and accrual basis
- Custom reports

### Fund Accounting

Maintain compliance across grants, programs, and restricted funds.

Included items:

- Restricted fund tracking
- Grant reporting support
- Program-level financial reporting
- Revenue recognition
- Fund balance monitoring

### Financial Visibility

Understand trends, risks, and opportunities before they become problems.

Included items:

- Variance analysis
- Benchmarking analysis
- Cash flow analysis
- General ledger management
- Financial health monitoring

### Accounting Operations

Create dependable financial systems that leadership can trust.

Included items:

- Timely bookkeeping
- Bank reconciliations
- Internal accountant oversight
- Chart of accounts maintenance
- Month-end close procedures

Closing copy:

Accurate accounting is the foundation of every strong nonprofit organization.

When leaders have confidence in their numbers, they can focus less on financial uncertainty and more on advancing their mission.
