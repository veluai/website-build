# Grant Advisory & Compliance Image Brief

## Purpose

This brief defines the visual asset strategy for the Grant Advisory & Compliance service page.

The image plan should support the approved page copy by reinforcing Funding Confidence, nonprofit stewardship, grant financial visibility, and professional advisory support. It is intended to guide future image generation, image selection, alt text development, and image inventory work.

This brief does not create final images, image URLs, schema, or final implemented alt text.

## Page Context

- Page name: Grant Advisory & Compliance
- URL: `/services/grant-advisory-compliance`
- Primary outcome: Funding Confidence
- Secondary outcome: Sustainable Growth
- Primary CTA: https://velu.us/get-started/

## Visual Direction

Imagery should communicate:

- Grant funding confidence
- Financial visibility
- Grant budgeting and planning
- Reporting and reimbursements
- Restricted funding and compliance
- Nonprofit stewardship
- Professional advisory support

Imagery should avoid looking like:

- Generic tax preparation
- Generic small business accounting
- Grant writing only
- Legal compliance only
- Overly technical government paperwork

The page should feel like a nonprofit financial advisory service, not a tax filing page, law firm compliance page, or grant writing service page. Visuals should make the financial side of grant funding feel clearer, more organized, and more manageable for nonprofit leaders.

## Visual Consistency Requirements

- Should align visually with current Outsourced Accounting and Client Advisory service pages.
- Should feel professional, modern, clean, nonprofit-focused, and advisory-oriented.
- Should use warm office, financial planning, dashboard, reporting, and collaboration cues where appropriate.
- Should avoid cluttered compliance imagery or intimidating government paperwork.
- Should reinforce Velu as a nonprofit accounting and advisory firm, not a generic CPA firm or grant writing agency.
- Should support the page hierarchy from outcome to capability to service to deliverable.
- Supporting illustrations should use a clean white-background Velu illustration style unless a different production treatment is separately approved.
