# Homepage Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the Velu homepage architecture, positioning, messaging, SEO role, conversion strategy, and schema role.

The homepage should establish the firm's nonprofit accounting and advisory positioning, introduce Velu's outcome framework, build trust, introduce core services, and direct qualified nonprofit visitors toward the primary get-started conversion path.

## Scope

This framework applies to the Velu homepage at:

https://velu.us/

It governs homepage messaging, structure, service presentation, lead magnet treatment, conversion strategy, SEO positioning, and schema alignment.

## Homepage Role

The homepage should:

- Establish Velu as a nonprofit accounting and advisory firm.
- Explain the firm's outcome framework.
- Build trust.
- Introduce services.
- Direct qualified visitors to the get-started conversion path.
- Support nonprofit accounting and advisory topical authority.

The homepage should create a clear path from visitor problem to Velu's service model. It should connect nonprofit financial challenges to Velu's outcomes and then to the relevant service categories.

## Homepage Positioning

The homepage is not:

- A generic CPA firm homepage
- A tax-only homepage
- A bookkeeping-only homepage
- A general small-business accounting homepage

The homepage is:

- A nonprofit accounting and advisory services homepage

Homepage copy, headings, imagery, schema, and calls to action should reinforce nonprofit specialization and avoid drifting into generic accounting positioning.

## Standard Homepage Structure

The current and intended homepage structure is:

1. Hero
2. Financial Growth Framework / outcome framework
3. Nonprofit pain points
4. Service introduction
5. Collaboration or trust-building section
6. Lead magnet
7. Reviews
8. CTA

This structure should guide future homepage updates unless Tyler Wilcox approves a material change.

## Core Homepage Message

The guiding homepage message is:

Velu helps nonprofit leaders build the financial clarity, confidence, and infrastructure they need to make stronger decisions, manage funding responsibly, and grow sustainably.

Future homepage copy should preserve this message even when individual headlines, section labels, or calls to action change.

## Outcome Framework Placement

The homepage should introduce:

- Financial Foundation
- Program-Finance Connection
- Funding Confidence
- Sustainable Growth

These outcomes explain why Velu's services matter. They should be presented before or alongside service details so visitors understand the leadership value behind the work.

## Service Introduction Standard

Homepage services:

- Outsourced Accounting
- Client Advisory Services
- Grant Advisory & Compliance

Do not present Strategic Financial Planning as a standalone service unless approved.

Service introductions should connect each service to the relevant outcome pillar. Outsourced Accounting should support Financial Foundation. Client Advisory Services should support Program-Finance Connection. Grant Advisory & Compliance should support Funding Confidence. All services should connect to Sustainable Growth as the long-term result.

## Lead Magnet Standard

Current lead magnet:

- The First Steps to Start a Nonprofit

The lead magnet supports early-stage nonprofit education and authority building. It may function as a secondary conversion action, but it should not replace the primary get-started conversion path for qualified prospects.

Educational lead magnet markup should follow the `CreativeWork` guidance in the schema standard when the resource is present on the live page.

## CTA Standard

Primary CTA:

https://velu.us/get-started/

The homepage should keep this conversion path clear. Secondary actions may support learning, trust building, or lead magnet engagement, but they should not obscure the primary path for qualified nonprofit prospects.

## Schema Connection

Homepage schema standards are governed by:

- [Schema Markup Standard](2-schema-markup-standard.md)

Homepage schema should normally include:

- `WebSite`
- `WebPage`
- `AccountingService`
- `OfferCatalog`
- `AggregateRating`
- `CreativeWork`
- `ScheduleAction`

The homepage should preserve root-domain schema at https://velu.us/ and use the approved organization identity for Velu.

## Future AI Agent Instructions

- Preserve root-domain homepage schema at https://velu.us/.
- Preserve nonprofit accounting and advisory positioning.
- Do not shift homepage toward generic CPA or tax positioning.
- Keep services connected to the outcome framework.
- Keep the homepage conversion path clear.
